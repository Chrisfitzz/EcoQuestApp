    - Summary:
      The design philosophy of EcoQuest is to gamify
      sustainable activity through local (missions) which reward users with badges and XP.
      This involves a backend with users that contain emails, passwords, and an xp stat.
      The app can locally handle the calculation for levels and badges.
      Context wrappers track user xp and log in status throughout the app.
      User data and local services are displayed via components on webpages.
      Webpages are routed together via App.js and the NavigationBar component allows
      users to move between pages anywhere in the app.
